ABOUT ME

Hi there! I'm Joel Cisneros, an aspiring software engineer/full stack web developer. I was born and raised in Orange County, CA and I am currently 18 years old embarking on my software developer journey. I graduated from Tarbut V' Torah Community Day School for highschool and decided self-study and a coding bootcamp was a more suitable option than traditional junior university for what I wish to do. I have been a tech 'geek' and very savvy with technologies most of my life.

Early on in my childhood I was introduced to video games, mobile technologies (apps, games, etc), and PC's. I was hooked the moment I started and always had a knack for solving whatever issue my technologies would provide. During highschool I dabbled in a little bit of code, attempting to make my own primitve video games to enjoy during lunchtime. However, I didn't fully delve into coding until the end of my senior year. At that point, I had been taught to think very logically by my parents and had natural problem-solving intuition.

I started my coding journey by taking Harvard's online CS50 class through their youtube videos. David Malan is an outstanding professor who taught me C++, several theoretical computer science topics, and more. After that, I decided to fully commit to becoming a software engineer and learned my first two languages (disregarding C++): HTML and CSS. Now I am enrolled in Learning Fuze's prep course and am learning JavaScript on the side to hopefully take their full immersion course so that I can achieve the higher level of education that I am looking for to achieve my dreams!
